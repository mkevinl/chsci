About

Central Intelligence (CI) is a bi-weekly news program created by the broadcast media students of Central High School in Springfield, MO. C.I., now in its 12th year, focuses on informing the students and staff of Central High School about current issues and school policies affecting teens.
Our goal is to be the voice of Central both to the Springfield community and the nation.

Mission

Central Intelligence is the premier student-directed, student-focused news program. We are committed to accuracy. We believe in being the voice of the Central High School students, staff and community. We are the voice of Central High School.
